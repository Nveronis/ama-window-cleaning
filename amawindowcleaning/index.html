Owner & Operator

Meet Austin

Hi, I'm Austin Ayotte, the founder of this business. A student at Western New England who turned a strong work ethic and a passion for doing things right into a window cleaning company built from the ground up. I launched this business at the start of 2023 with the simple belief that every customer deserves to be treated like a priority. That means showing up on time, communicating clearly, and not calling a job done until the results speak for themselves. No streaks, no shortcuts.

What sets us apart isn't just the clean windows; it's the experience. I know what it feels like to hire someone and wonder if they'll actually show up or care about your home the way you do. I built this company to be the kind of service I would want to hire myself: reliable, honest, and genuinely invested in getting it right.

Whether you're a homeowner looking to brighten up your space or a business wanting to make a great first impression, I would love to help. Reach out today — I'm always happy to chat, answer questions, or put together a free quote.
